After work experience in Sound techniques and Photography, I progressively dived into the realm of digital products and graduated with a Master of Information Architecture from the Ecole Nationale Supérieure de Lyon in France.

I got different titles such as UX Designer and Project Manager until I progressively trained myself in Data engineering and Solution architecture throughout projects both in the private and non-profit sectors.
I have had the chance to be involved in a variety of domains and companies such as:

  • Digital Humanities at Europeana Foundation
  • Reporting services for the media industry at RTL Nederland
  • Machine-learning applied to Real-estate appraisal at Geophy
  • Data-engineering in a Banking context at Rabobank
  • App marketplace analytics at data.ai
  • Data-driven system design at Clear Global (former Translators without borders)
  • Aviation and airports data services at Aerlabs

Besides, I have designed and developed the Muzai platform. It is a streamlined data platform embedded with Computer vision features, dedicated to enhance digitized art collections with Linked Data and IIIF standards. I presented this project at the 2019 IIIF Conference in Göttingen, Germany. It is available at muzai.io.

I currently work at the World Food Program in Rome, Italy as a Consultant software engineer, focusing on the data pipelines which support the food security monitoring infrastructure. Part of this work can be seen through the HungerMap product, a live dashboard focused on the situation of 30+ countries.

Through my career, I have eventually lead small teams composed of designers and developers, and I am now looking to take on managerial positions such as Product Manager and apply my holistic approach and coordination skills further into the development of data-driven products.

Guided by my initial training in Information Architecture I tend to follow design principles, while taking great care of gathering and considering every technical aspects based on my experience with Agile/SCRUM techniques.
My drive is to define and structure with all due respect the work at hand. It ensures that both time and resources are allocated wisely, and with each component delivery, builds up development momentum and brings more opportunities for new features.

I genuinely believe that team spirit and consistent coordination are necessary to walk the walk and deliver great digital products supported by sound engineering and smooth user-experience.
